# PRODUCT.md

## Register
brand — this is a portfolio site, design IS the product.

## Product Purpose
Personal portfolio for Justin Chen (student at The Harker School). The site centers on his shipped and work-in-progress projects, presented with a playful, distinctive feel rather than as a generic developer portfolio.

## Users
- Recruiters and program reviewers evaluating Justin's work
- Peers and collaborators discovering projects
- Future Justin, as a living archive

## Brand & Tone
- Playful, tactile, memorable
- Projects are the centerpiece, each given an interactive entry point rather than a flat grid
- Harker School background surfaces as part of the story, not a credential wall

## Anti-References (do NOT do)
- Arial as a header / display font
- Em dashes inside body copy
- `---` rule dividers or `01`-style numbered section headers in the rendered UI
- Default or stock logos given by tooling — wait for explicit logo input
- Generic SaaS-cream aesthetic; identical card grids; hero-metric template
- Anything that reads as "AI made that" at a glance

## Strategic Principles
1. The project showcase is the centerpiece. Hero and Contact frame it; they do not compete with it.
2. Every project is a tactile, interactive entry point — not a thumbnail in a grid.
3. No final design decisions (logo, fonts, palette, showcase A vs B) without Justin's explicit confirmation.
4. Content lives in code. This is not a CMS-backed site and not a blog.

## Open Decisions (deferred)
- Showcase direction: Option A (rotating 3D namecards) vs Option B (floating soft-edged screens)
- Tone sub-flavor: minimal-playful vs maximalist-playful vs retro-playful
- Palette, font picks, logo — all pending sign-off
- Dark vs light vs both — pending

## Tech Context
Next.js 16 App Router · TypeScript · Tailwind v4 · React Three Fiber 9 · drei. R3F is the primary differentiator and will carry the showcase.
